Privacy policy.

Effective May 1, 2026. We collect the minimum information needed to run Tester.io, and we never sell it.

What we collect

  • Account data — name, email, password hash, workspace and team membership.
  • Test artifacts — spec files, screenshots, videos, console logs, and traces that you upload via test runs.
  • Usage telemetry — anonymous, aggregated metrics on which features are used. You can opt out in Workspace → Settings.
  • Billing data — handled exclusively by Stripe. We store only the last 4 digits and brand.
  • Support correspondence — email threads and screenshots you send us, retained for 24 months.

What we do not collect

  • We do not place advertising cookies or third-party trackers.
  • We do not sell, lease, or rent your data — ever.
  • We do not train AI models on your test artifacts.
  • We do not share data with sub-processors not listed in our DPA.

Where data lives

Customer data is stored in your chosen region: EU (Frankfurt) or US (Virginia). It does not leave that region without your explicit configuration. Backups are encrypted at rest with AES-256 and replicated within the same region.

Retention

  • Test artifacts: 90 days by default — configurable up to 7 years on Enterprise.
  • Account data: retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of cancellation.
  • Aggregated telemetry: retained indefinitely in anonymized form for product improvement.

Your rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and equivalent regulations, you have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data at any time. Self-serve tools live in Account → Privacy. For complex requests, email privacy@tester.io and we'll respond within 30 days.

Contact

Our Data Protection Officer can be reached at dpo@tester.io or by post: Tester.io GmbH, Torstraße 142, 10119 Berlin, Germany.

This page changes infrequently. Material changes will be communicated by email at least 30 days before they take effect. The complete version history is maintained in our public policies repository.
