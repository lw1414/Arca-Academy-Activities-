How we build the platform

  • SSO & SAML on every paid tier — Okta, Azure AD, Google, JumpCloud, custom OIDC.
  • SCIM provisioning for automated user/group lifecycle.
  • Audit logs exported to S3, GCS, or Splunk. Immutable, signed, 7-year retention available.
  • Role-based access control with least-privilege defaults and per-workspace policies.
  • Encryption: TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256-GCM at rest. Customer-managed keys (KMS) on Enterprise.
  • Network: IP allowlisting, VPC peering, and private-link options for enterprise customers.
  • Secret scanning: every PR runs through a hardened secret-detection pipeline before merge.
  • Dependency hygiene: SBOM published per release; CVE alerts triaged within 24 hours.

How we run the platform

  • Hosted on AWS in EU (Frankfurt) and US (Virginia). Customer data stays in your chosen region.
  • All production access is just-in-time, MFA-required, and logged with session recording.
  • Quarterly disaster-recovery drills with documented RPO < 5 minutes, RTO < 1 hour.
  • Vendor risk assessment for every sub-processor; the list is in our DPA.
  • Background checks and security training for every employee with production access, refreshed annually.

Responsible disclosure

If you believe you've found a security vulnerability, please email security@tester.io with reproduction steps. Our PGP key is published at /.well-known/security.txt. We acknowledge within 24 hours, triage within 72, and pay bounties through HackerOne ranging from $250 to $25,000 depending on impact.

Want our SOC 2 report, pen-test summary, or vendor security questionnaire? Email security@tester.io — typical turnaround is one business day under NDA.
