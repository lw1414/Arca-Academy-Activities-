Data processing addendum.

Effective May 1, 2026. This DPA is incorporated by reference into our Terms of Service. It governs how Tester.io processes personal data on your behalf.

Roles

For purposes of GDPR, UK GDPR, and similar regulations: you are the Controller; Tester.io GmbH ("Tester.io") is the Processor. Tester.io processes Customer Personal Data only to provide the service per documented instructions (the Terms and your in-product configuration).

Scope of processing

  • Subject matter: provision of the Tester.io platform.
  • Duration: the term of your subscription, plus retention windows specified in our Privacy Policy.
  • Nature: hosting, storage, transmission, display, and processing of Customer Personal Data.
  • Types of personal data: identifiers (name, email), authentication data, usage telemetry, any personal data you choose to include in test artifacts.
  • Categories of data subjects: your employees, contractors, end users (where personal data appears in test fixtures).

Sub-processors

Tester.io engages the following sub-processors to deliver the service. We notify customers by email at least 30 days before adding or replacing a sub-processor.

Hosting

Amazon Web Services

EU (Frankfurt) or US (Virginia) regions per customer election. Standard Contractual Clauses in place.

Email transactional

Resend

For account, billing, and welcome emails. EU data residency.

Payments

Stripe

Card data never touches Tester.io systems. PCI DSS Level 1.

Customer support

Plain (plain.com)

Inbound support channel. EU data residency available.

Error monitoring

Sentry (self-hosted)

Run on our own AWS account in the customer's region. No third-party access.

Analytics

Plausible (self-hosted)

Anonymous, cookieless. Self-hosted on our own infrastructure.

Security measures

Tester.io implements the technical and organizational measures described in our Security page, which form the agreed-upon Annex II of this DPA. These include encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256), MFA-enforced access, immutable audit logs, and regular third-party assessments.

International transfers

Where Customer Personal Data is transferred outside the EEA/UK/Switzerland, Tester.io relies on the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (2021/914), the UK Addendum, and Swiss equivalent. We perform a transfer impact assessment for each sub-processor and apply supplementary measures as needed.

Data subject rights

Tester.io provides tools in Account → Privacy for you to fulfill data subject requests (access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction). For complex cases, we assist within reasonable cooperation, charged at cost.

Audits

You may audit Tester.io's compliance with this DPA once per twelve-month period, on at least 60 days' notice, during business hours, subject to mutually-agreed confidentiality terms. Our most recent SOC 2 Type II report and ISO 27001 certificate generally satisfy this requirement.

Breach notification

Tester.io will notify Customer without undue delay, and in any case within 72 hours, after becoming aware of a Personal Data Breach affecting Customer Personal Data. Notice will include the categories of data subjects and records affected, the likely consequences, and the measures taken or proposed.

Term & deletion

This DPA remains in effect for the duration of the Terms. On termination, Tester.io deletes or returns all Customer Personal Data within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable law.

A signed counterpart of this DPA is available on request — dpo@tester.io.
