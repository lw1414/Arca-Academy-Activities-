How we hire

  1. Application review — within 5 business days. Real humans, no resume scanners.
  2. Intro chat (45 min) — with the hiring manager, no whiteboard.
  3. Take-home (≤4 hrs, paid) — a scoped problem from a real PR we shipped.
  4. Team day (½ day) — pairing, system design conversation, and lunch with the team you'd join.
  5. Offer — full transparency on comp, equity, level, and what we expect in the first 90 days.
